Hearing Aids

A hearing aid is a device that amplifies sound for a person with hearing loss. The ultimate goal of a hearing aid is to improve the speech understanding ability of the person and to help them in communication which leads to the normal participation of the person in day-to-day life.

The selection of hearing aids should be based on the type and severity of hearing loss, listening needs, and lifestyle.

TMC is expertise in hearing aids. We have collaborations with leading hearing instrument manufacturers like Phonak, Oticon and Seimens, etc. We follow a patient-oriented approach for advising hearing aids based on their needs and requirements.
