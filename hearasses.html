Hearing Assessment

Hearing loss is defined as the partial or complete inability to hear sounds in one or both the ears.

Audiologists are health care professionals who are trained and expertised in the assessment and management of hearing loss and other associated disorders.

Milestones specializes in evaluating your Hearing capacity and status with Pure Tone Audiometry, Speech audiometry and other tests. The Audiologists at our Clinics will guide you through the best products and services to provide you with maximum benefits from the Hearing Aid and help you in the whole process of selection and usage.

The services provided at our clinic to assess the overall hearing status, site of lesion, severity etc are

  • Otoscopic examination
  • Pure Tone Audiometry
  • Speech audiometry
  • Tympanometry
  • Acoustic reflex testing
  • Auditory brainstem response measurements(ABR/BERA)
  • Otoacoustic emissions
  • Other special tests

Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA)

Pure Tone Audiometry is one of the basic and gold standard hearing tests used to identify the hearing ability of an individual. It helps us in determining the degree, type and configuration of a hearing loss. The patient is asked to respond to the pure tone stimuli presented at different frequencies. Both Air conduction & bone conduction thresholds are detected by this test.

Immittance Audiometry

Immittance audiometry is a test of the middle ear. It is a very quick, simple, reliable and relatively objective method for determining middle-ear function and status, detecting the presence of middle ear fluid or eardrum perforation, etc

Speech Audiometry Test (SAT)

Speech Audiometry is a test used to determine the speech understanding ability of an individual. It determines the threshold (lowest level) at which speech is audible and intelligible.

Oto Acoustic Emissions test

OAEs are a test of the inner ear. Oto acoustic emission test helps to assess the functioning of the hair cells in the cochlea. OAE test is a simple, quick, and non-invasive test, for detecting hearing problems in new-born babies, children who are too young to cooperate, or in difficult-to-test populations.

BERA test

The brainstem evoked response audiometry (BERA) is an objective testing method for evaluating hearing threshold and diagnosing auditory nerve lesions. BERA test is an effective tool for assessing hearing thresholds of difficult to test populations like newborns, children, bedridden patients, mentally challenged patients, patients with behavioral problems etc.
